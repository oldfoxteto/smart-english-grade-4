AI ENGLISH MASTER - PROMPTS LIBRARY

=== LEVEL ASSESSMENT PROMPTS ===
Prompt 1: "Ask: What's your name and why do you want to learn English?"
Prompt 2: "Ask: How would you introduce yourself in English?"
Prompt 3: "Ask: What's your current English level (beginner/intermediate/advanced)?"
Prompt 4: "Ask: What's your main goal (travel/study/business/conversation)?"
Prompt 5: "Ask: How much time can you dedicate daily?"

=== LESSON GENERATION PROMPTS ===
Vocabulary Lesson: "Create a 15-minute vocabulary lesson about {topic} for {level} level. Include 10 new words, definitions, and example sentences."

Grammar Lesson: "Explain {grammar_topic} for {level} level in simple terms. Provide 5 examples and 3 practice exercises."

Conversation Lesson: "Create a realistic dialogue about {scenario} for {level} level. Include cultural notes and common phrases."

Listening Lesson: "Design a listening exercise about {topic} for {level} level. Include audio transcript and comprehension questions."

Reading Lesson: "Create a reading passage about {topic} for {level} level. Include vocabulary list and comprehension questions."

Writing Lesson: "Design a writing task about {topic} for {level} level. Provide template and evaluation criteria."

=== EXERCISE GENERATION PROMPTS ===
Multiple Choice: "Create 5 multiple choice questions about {topic} for {level} level. Include explanations."

Fill in the Blank: "Create 10 fill-in-the-blank sentences about {grammar_topic} for {level} level."

Matching: "Create matching exercise for {vocabulary_set} with 10 items."

Speaking: "Design speaking practice prompts about {topic} for {level} level."

=== CORRECTION PROMPTS ===
Grammar Correction: "Correct this sentence: '{user_sentence}'. Explain the error and provide 3 correct alternatives."

Pronunciation: "Analyze pronunciation of '{word}'. Provide phonetic transcription and practice tips."

Writing Feedback: "Evaluate this writing: '{user_text}'. Score grammar, vocabulary, structure, and coherence."

=== MOTIVATION PROMPTS ===
Encouragement: "User completed {lesson}. Provide positive feedback and next steps."

Streak Reminder: "User has {streak_days} day streak. Send encouraging message."

Level Up: "User reached {new_level}. Celebrate achievement and explain benefits."

=== PERSONALIZATION PROMPTS ===
Adaptive Content: "User scored {score}% on {topic}. Adjust difficulty to {new_level}."

Weakness Analysis: "User struggles with {weak_areas}. Create targeted practice exercises."

Learning Style: "User prefers {learning_style}. Adapt content to be more {style_description}."
